 A BRIEF OVERVIEW

Horticultural crop production has surpassed the food grain crop production for the first time in the history of Indian Agriculture. The food industry of India is ranked among the largest in the country and 5th largest in terms of production, consumption and export.Keeping the importance of organic farming, to increase use of organic manure utilization for crop production, there is an urgent need to produce and supply organic manure to the farming community.It is planned to extend the organic village site programme to hobli level.

The objective of the scheme is to increase the agriculture production and productivity by adoption of Farm Mechanisation. The scheme is being implemented with five different components namely supply of Hi-tech Agriculture implements, and supply of newly designed agricultural implements.
